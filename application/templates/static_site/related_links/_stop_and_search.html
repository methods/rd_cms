Arrests
In 2017/18, Black people were over 3 times as likely to be arrested as White people. And overall, men were over 5 times as likely to be arrested as women.

Victims of crime
In 2017/18, a higher percentage of people from the Mixed and Asian ethnic groups said they were victims of crime compared with White people.

Police workforce Between 2007 and 2018, the percentage of police officers from the Asian, Black, Mixed and Other ethnic groups combined increased from 3.9% to 6.6%.
