Prosecutions and convictions
In 2017, the conviction ratio was lowest for defendants in the Black (78.7%) and Mixed (79.0%) ethnic groups, and highest in the White ethnic group (85.3%).

Victims of crime
In 2017/18, a higher percentage of people from the Mixed and Asian ethnic groups said they were victims of crime compared with White people.

Stop and search
In 2017/18, Black people were 9 and a half times as likely to be stopped and searched as White people.
