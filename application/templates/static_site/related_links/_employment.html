Employment by occupation
In 2017, the Black, Mixed and Other ethnic groups had a higher than average percentage of their workforce in ‘elementary’ occupations, the lowest skilled type of occupation.

Unemployment
In 2017, just under 4% of White people were unemployed, compared with 8% of people from all other ethnic groups combined.

Employment by sector
The public administration, education and health sector employed the highest percentage of workers in every ethnic group except the Pakistani/Bangladeshi group.
