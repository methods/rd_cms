Household income
Black households were most likely to have a weekly income of less than £400, and Indian households most likely to have a weekly income of £1000 or more.

Income distribution
76% of Pakistani households fell into the 2 lowest income quintiles (after housing costs) – the highest percentage out of all ethnic groups.

Employment by occupation
In 2017, the Black, Mixed and Other ethnic groups had a higher than average percentage of their workforce in ‘elementary’ occupations, the lowest skilled type of occupation.
