Ethnicity facts and figures

In the UK, 87% of people are White, and 13% belong to a Black, Asian, Mixed or Other ethnic minority.

Use this service to find information about the different experiences of people from a wide variety of ethnic backgrounds. It gathers data collected by government in one place, making it available to the public, specialists and charities.
