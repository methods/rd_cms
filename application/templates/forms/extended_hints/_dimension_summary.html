This data shows that:

  • 63% of households in England owned their own homes in the 2 years to March 2018
  • from November 2018 to November 2019, 63.3% of people in England aged 16 and over were ‘physically active’ – they did 150 minutes or more of moderate intensity physical activity a week
  • 17% of households in England lived in social housing (they rented their home from a local authority or housing association)
  • White Gypsy and Roma, and Traveller of Irish Heritage pupils had the highest school exclusion rates (both permanent and temporary) in the 2017 to 2018 school year
  • the percentage of prison officers who did not state their ethnicity went up from 9.8% to 30.1%, in part due to changes in data collection from new staff
